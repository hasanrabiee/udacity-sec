# Describe GuardDuty findings that were detected related to the brute force attack
AWS GuardDuty is a threat detection service that continuously monitors malicious or unauthorized behavior in your AWS environment. In the context of a brute force attack, GuardDuty would typically detect multiple unsuccessful attempts to log in to your EC2 instance or application, signaling a potential attack
